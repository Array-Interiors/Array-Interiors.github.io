Bedrooms

The bedroom is the perfect space for relaxation and rejuvenation. Various aspects like right color, furnishings, textures, flooring, and furniture to create a tranquil space have to be considered in home decor.

Being the most private section of the home it should be designed with a personal touch. Storage is a primary concern and right wardrobe and cupboards need to be integrated into the design.

Accessories and lighting can add perfect finesse to bedroom designs.
